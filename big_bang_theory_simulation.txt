
Competing Correlation Model for Identifying the Best Solution to The Big Bang Theory

Simulation Steps:

1. Literature Review Bot:
   - Collects and analyzes relevant research papers.
   - Produces a summary report of findings.

2. Data Analysis Bot:
   - Processes astronomical data.
   - Identifies key patterns and anomalies.

3. Hypothesis Generation Bot:
   - Creates a set of hypotheses based on literature and data insights.
   - Describes each hypothesis in detail.

4. Simulation and Testing Bot:
   - Simulates scenarios for each hypothesis.
   - Tests hypotheses against observed data.

5. Output Comparison:
   - Compares outputs from all bots.
   - Evaluates the accuracy and validity of each hypothesis.

6. Best Solution Identification:
   - Identifies the most accurate and well-supported theory based on comparisons.
